Jyotiska Khasnabish

A developer based in Bangalore, India, mostly working in Python, JavaScript and sometimes Go. I currently work as Data Engineer with GSN Games. Previously, I worked with DataWeave, a Bangalore based startup that provides pricing and retail intelligence for e-commerce vendors, as a Senior Engineer in their Products Team. Before joining DataWeave, I completed my Masters with Data Science specialization from IIIT-Bangalore and finished my Masters thesis during my internship in Infosys Labs.

Whenever I find time, I work on few side projects and contribute to open source. My contributions can be found on my Github profile.
